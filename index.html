About Me

Hello world! My name is Brian and I originally thought I was going to be an accountant. Having graduated from university with a degree in Business Economics, I started off with an accounting internship. I soon realized that I did not enjoy what I was doing. The work felt mundane and was pretty much like a checklist.

Thereafter, a friend suggested that I try out coding. I decided to give it a shot and I haven't looked back ever since. It's exciting how there is a countless number of technologies out there to learn and mess around with. I've recently started tinkering with React and would love to build front-end applications using React.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending my free time watching new shows on Netflix or playing video games such as Valorant or League of Legends.
