About Me

Hello world! My name is Brian and I graduated from the University of California, Irvine, with a degree in Business Economics and a focus in accounting. After completing an internship at an accounting firm, I realized I wasn't passionate about what I was doing and decided to change careers.

I switched to software engineering and I haven't looked back ever since. The countless amount of technologies out there to learn and mess around with is exciting. I specialize in building dynamic websites using JavaScript or React and creating a pleasant user experience.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending my free time watching new shows on Netflix or playing video games such as Valorant and League of Legends.
